Meditation Centre Development

We have relocated part of our public programs and suspended others in due respect to a order given by The City of Edmonton to cease offering public services at our current premise due to bylaw restrictions.

Our goal is to purchase or build a facility in a location where we have the permission for Religious Assembly so that we can resume our meditation programs, Dhamma discussions, children's programs, youth programs, mindfulness development programs, stress relief programs, counselling, retreats and daily sermons, etc. that we offered, without any hindrance. We also need to ensure there is sufficient space for hosting our spiritual, cultural and community programs in it.

This is financially much more challenging than acquiring a residential place. However, we are confident that this challenge can be overcome, and enable this meditation centre to continue its public service, thanking to the commitment and generous support of our community.
